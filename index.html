"One Man’s Trash is Another Man’s Treasure".

Share the Warmth is an initiative aimed at bridging this gap. Statistically more than a third of the world's population lives in poverty with no real means for basic necessities including clothing. People often throw away lightly used clothing which could be beneficial for the lesser privileged.
This happens mainly because they don’t have the time or avenues to donate.
Our goal is to become an effective mediator, in the Triangle area, thereby benefitting both parties.
